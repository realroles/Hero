Privacy Policy

At Real Role AI, accessible from https://realrole.store, your privacy is very important to us. This Privacy Policy explains what type of information we collect, how we use it, and how we protect your personal data.

Information We Collect

  • General usage data such as browser type, device information, and IP address.
  • Messages entered into the chatbot (processed in real-time, not stored permanently).
  • Contact details (only if you voluntarily reach out to us).

How We Use Your Information

We use collected data for purposes such as:

  • Improving AI chatbot accuracy and user experience.
  • Analyzing website performance and traffic patterns.
  • Delivering relevant content and advertisements.

Cookies and Tracking

Real Role AI uses cookies to enhance user experience. Cookies may be used to remember user preferences, measure traffic, and provide personalized content or advertisements. Users may choose to disable cookies through their browser settings, though some features may not work properly.

Advertising Partners

Our website may display advertisements served by third-party advertising networks. These partners may use cookies and tracking technologies to provide relevant ads based on user interests and browsing activity. Any information collected through advertisements is subject to the policies of those third parties.

Data Protection

We respect your privacy and do not sell, trade, or rent personal information. Appropriate security measures are applied to protect your data. However, please note that no online service can guarantee absolute security.

Children’s Privacy

Real Role AI does not knowingly collect personal information from children under the age of 13. If you believe your child has shared such data with us, please contact us immediately and we will take necessary action.

Your Privacy Choices

Users can disable cookies through browser settings or use opt-out tools provided by advertising networks. This allows you to control how personalized ads are displayed to you.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any updates will be reflected on this page with a new revision date.

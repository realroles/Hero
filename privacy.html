Privacy Policy

At Real Role AI, accessible from https://realrole.store, we respect your privacy. Our website is a simple AI chatbot that works directly from the browser. We do not collect, store, or share any personal information from users.

Information Collection

Real Role AI does not require you to create an account or provide personal data. Any text you type into the chatbot is processed in real-time and is not stored by us.

Our website may use cookies to improve performance and deliver relevant advertisements in the future. Third-party advertising networks may use cookies and tracking technologies to display ads based on your browsing interests. You can disable cookies through your browser settings if you prefer.

Data Protection

Since we do not store user data, there is no risk of your personal information being sold or misused. Any limited technical data collected (such as general analytics) is used only to improve website functionality.

Children’s Privacy

Real Role AI is suitable for all users, but we do not knowingly collect information from children under 13. Parents and guardians are encouraged to supervise online activity.
